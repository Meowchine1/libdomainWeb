Test stands infrastructure development has begun

Our team has started developing the architecture of test stands. It is planned to use OpenLDAP and Samba LDAP services for testing. This solution will allow us to test the product under development in conditions close to real ones and to deploy the test environment faster.

To simplify the testing and reporting process, it was decided to use several Docker-based test environments.
